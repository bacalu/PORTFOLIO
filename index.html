ABOUT ME


My name is MARTA CRISTHA BACALU and I study Architecture and Urban planning. As a dedicated student of architecture and urban planning, my career objective is to harness the power of architecture as a catalyst for positive societal change. I am deeply passionate about the sociological component of architecture and its potential to elevate and transform the standard of living for communities and individuals alike.

My passion for the sociological aspects of architecture, combined with my dedication to creating spaces that enhance the quality of life, drives my pursuit of a career where I can contribute to meaningful architectural solutions. I am eager to apply my knowledge and skills to transform communities and create spaces that leave a lasting positive impact on society.

I started learning graphic design as a hobby, and soon, I discovered I could take this passion further and exert myself. I was for a year the coordinator of the design department of the Architecture Students' Association, also, the social manager and the content creator for an informative Instagram account.

I am creative, I love working on myself and my expansion through new experiences.

My favourite colour is #6f9583
